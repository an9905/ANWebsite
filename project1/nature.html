parks

If you are visiting Queens, you can not skip over our famous parks! We are home to many different parks that are open year round. One is Forest Park which is HUGE, specifically 538 acres big. We also have Queens Botanical Garden which is one of our biggest attractions as it is just beautiful. Lastly, we have Queensbridge Park home of many famous celebrities like Nas and Mobb Deep

beaches

Although people do not generally go to New York for their beaches, we do have some that are quite worth the visit. Rockaway beach, my personal favorite, offers a nice beach with a beautiful view of the gated communities. Another famous beach is Jones Beach which is known for its cold water. Lastly we have Hallet Cove Beach which I have never been to personally, but have heard good things about!
