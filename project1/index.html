Background

Queens is a borough of New York City, otherwise known as the City that never sleeps. It is the largest borough of New York City in area and is adjacent to the borough of Brooklyn. Queens is also the second-largest in population of the five New York City boroughs. It is also the most linguistically diverse place on Earth and is one of the most ethnically diverse counties in the United States.

I have lived in Queens for some years now, in a district called Whitestone. There is a lot of sites in Queens most of which I have visited. If you would like to see all of the different things Queens has to offer, click some of the links at the top of this page!
