culture

Queens is the most ethnically diverse urban area in the world, as residents often closely identify with their neighborhood rather than with the borough or city. The borough is a patchwork of dozens of unique neighborhoods, each with its own distinct identity. Representing over 100 nations and speaking 138 different languages, Queens has a neighborhood for anybody!
