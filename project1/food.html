Christina's Deli & Jackson Hole

Queens is home to many diverse resteraunts and eatery. We are famous for our bacon egg & cheese, but we also have a lot of flavor from all parts of the world. Near my house, is Christina's Deli the BEST deli in New York City. The menu contains 150 different sandwich combinations, and is just the best! In all, our food depends really on what place you want to visit that day, whether it be Chinese food, Indian food, or my personal favorite Mediteranean food!
